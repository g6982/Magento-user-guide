Odoo Magento 2 Connector

Magento Odoo integration is a necessity because the manual entry of data between the two platforms is simply not possible after a certain level of growth, where the data becomes bigger and more complex. Our Odoo-Magento 2 connector app automates your vital business processes and eliminates the need for manual data entry in Odoo by enabling bi-directional data exchange between your Magento 2 and Odoo. Also, this module saves your time by automatically & instantly synchronizing your catalogs, orders, customers, and inventory data from your online store in Magento 2 to Odoo. After installation of this module, there will be a rare need to login into Magento 2 admin panel for managing business operations. You can manage most of the operations from Odoo itself and that too with integrating inventory with other popular business apps like CRM, Purchase & Accounting.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

How many Magento2 stores can I connect with single Odoo instance?

The Odoo Magento2 Connector lets connect as many Magento2 stores as you want with a single Odoo Instance.

Does the Odoo Magento2 Connector allow two-way synchronization of Products?

Yes, you can perform export operations for Shipment, Invoice and Product Stock from Odoo to Magento2 stores as well as Import Products (Simple & Configurable), Stock, Price, etc. from Magento2 stores to Odoo.</p><p>However, you cannot export the products from Odoo to Magento2 stores. If the products are already present on Odoo as well as Magento2, you can establish synchronization between them using the connector.

On what basis are the product synchronized between Magento2 stores and Odoo?

The connector synchronizes products by matching the Internal Reference field in Odoo with the SKU field in Magento2 stores. It is mandatory to have a unique SKU or Internal Reference for a product.

What kind of orders get imported to Odoo from Magento2 stores and how can I control it?

Any order on Magento2 stores can be imported to Odoo if the connector has been configured correctly. Orders in Magento2 stores have different order statuses such as Processing, On hold, Completed & Pending Payment. In the connector, you can choose one or more order statuses and import orders only of your chosen statuses.

Can I automize certain synchronization tasks in Odoo?

Yes. You can automize the synchronization tasks in Odoo by configuring the Auto Cron Configuration from connector settings.

Can I send Email to customers from Odoo once shipment or invoice is done?

Yes. You can send the Emails to customers of your Magento2 stores directly from Odoo. Simply check the box of Shipment, Invoice or both from <b>Magento / Magento Stores / Main Website Store.</b>

Does the Magento2 Odoo connector support magento 2.2 patch versions?

No, Magento2 Odoo connector does not support Magento 2.2 patch versions.
